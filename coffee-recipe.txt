Ingredients (4 cold servings):
	- 4 cups milk
	- 1/2 cup chocolate syrup
	- 4 tablespoons cocoa powder
	- 4 tablespoons sugar
	- 1 1/4 tablespoon coffee
	- 4 small ice cubes (if needed)

Directions:
	1. Pour milk in a large vessel and add all the ingredients to it.

	2. Stir it till everything is mixed well.

	3. Pour the coffee in 4 cups and put an ice cube in each cup if needed.
